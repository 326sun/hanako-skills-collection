## Workflow 1. **Search**: Query Google Scholar and arXiv with relevant keywords. Use boolean operators for precision.
2. **Screen by abstract**: Filter by relevance, recency, and citation count. Discard clearly irrelevant papers.
3. **Screen by figures**: Scan figures/tables for methodological quality and result clarity.
4. **Organize by theme**: Group papers into thematic clusters. Identify consensus, controversy, and gaps. ## Output - Thematic summary with representative citations per cluster
- Key findings, methodological trends, and open questions
- Gap analysis: what is under-studied or contested
